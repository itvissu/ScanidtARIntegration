ScanditBarcodeCapture
=====================

.. module:: scandit.datacapture.barcode
  :hidden:

*ScanditBarcodeCapture* implements the barcode capture capabilities of the |sdk|. It supports reading a large number of different :type:`barcode symbologies<Symbology>`.  

.. note::

  Just getting started? Have a look at :doc:`our samples</run-samples>`, or directly :doc:`get started by adding barcode scanning to your app</get-started-barcode>`.
