ScanditParser
=============

.. module:: scandit.datacapture.parser
  :hidden:

*ScanditParser* implements the parsing capabilities of the |sdk|. It supports parsing a large number of different :doc:`data formats<formats>`.

  Just getting started? Have a look at :doc:`our samples</run-samples>`, or directly :doc:`get started by adding parsing to your app</get-started-parser>`.
